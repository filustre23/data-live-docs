# Episode 3: Getting data into Hex

Learn how to get your data into Hex via warehouse integration, file upload, and more.

#### In this episode[​](#in-this-episode "Direct link to In this episode")

Data, oh data, it surrounds us like a shimmering mist! In episode 3 of our Hex Foundations course, we continue our journey by venturing off into the realm of data connections. We show you how you can upload files to your Hex projects and introduce you to the Notebook view sidebar (fka Logic view sidebar). We also cover how to retrieve data from a data warehouse or cloud storage connection, and incorporate it into your project. Inching you closer and closer to Hex mastery!

* [Different ways to get your data into Hex](/docs/getting-started/connect-your-data)
* [Uploading files](/docs/connect-to-data/upload-files)
