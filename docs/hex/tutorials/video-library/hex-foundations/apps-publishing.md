# Episode 14: Apps and publishing

Learn how to build apps and share them with others

#### In this episode[​](#in-this-episode "Direct link to In this episode")

Welcome to episode 14 of our series on Hex fundamentals. In the final episode, we show you how Hex isn’t too dissimilar from most multiplayer games. We show how to leave comments for your teammates, jump in a project, make some edits, then turn all of that into an app once finished. It concludes with a demonstration of sharing apps and publishing them.

Hex is a collaborative workspace for exploratory analytics and data science. With Hex, teams can quickly reach insights in AI-powered notebooks using SQL, Python, & no-code, and instantly share their work with anyone.
