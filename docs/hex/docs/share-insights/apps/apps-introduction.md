# Apps introduction

Hex Apps let you arrange, format, and publish a selection of cells as a dashboard, report, or interactive data app.

info

* Available on all [pricing plans](https://hex.tech/pricing).
* Users will need **Can Edit** or higher [project permissions](/docs/collaborate/sharing-and-permissions/project-sharing#project-permissions) to build and publish Apps.
* Users will need **Can View App** or higher project permissions to view a published App.

## What are Apps?[​](#what-are-apps "Direct link to What are Apps?")

Apps are a curated selection of cells from your project that you can publish and share with stakeholders. Building an App is the fastest way to create evergreen dashboards, ad hoc reports, and internal tools or data apps from a notebook or query. Turn any analysis into a beautifully-formatted deliverable in a matter of seconds.

## Select cells from your project[​](#select-cells-from-your-project "Direct link to Select cells from your project")

From the [App builder](/docs/share-insights/apps/app-builder) you can choose the input parameters, text, and display [cells](/docs/explore-data) that you want to show your stakeholders, while hiding the underlying code in your [Notebook view](/docs/explore-data/notebook-view/develop-your-notebook). Of course, if you *want* to show off your Python and SQL cells in your App, you can!

## Publish and iterate[​](#publish-and-iterate "Direct link to Publish and iterate")

[Publishing](/docs/share-insights/apps/publish-and-share-apps) your project makes the current version available on your App. You can continue editing and iterating on versions of your project in the Notebook view without affecting the published version that's live in your App. When you're ready to push the latest changes, you can re-publish to update your App.

## Share with stakeholders[​](#share-with-stakeholders "Direct link to Share with stakeholders")

Apps are only visible to people with whom you have shared your project. You can grant someone **App-Only** [project permissions](/docs/collaborate/sharing-and-permissions/sharing-permissions), to allow them to view and comment only on the published App. This is great for sharing with non-technical stakeholders or clients.

## Build your knowledge Library[​](#build-your-knowledge-library "Direct link to Build your knowledge Library")

Adding an [Endorsed status](/docs/organize-content/statuses-categories#endorsed-statuses) to an App marks it as part of your organization's knowledge base, and makes it easily discoverable by other members of your workspace within the [Library](/docs/organize-content/statuses-categories#library).
